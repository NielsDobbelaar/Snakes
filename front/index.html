    How to play?
    • The goal is to gain as many points as possible by growing your tail the longest. Avoid crashing into other snakes, the walls, or yourself!
    • You can only gain points when there are other snakes on the field.
    • Use the arrow keys to move your snake around the game.
    • See if you can make it to the top of the highscore list!
    • If you want to play with friends, share the link to this page with them!
    • If you're interested in the code, you can find it on My GitHub.
